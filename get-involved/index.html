Get Involved

To help accomplish its mission, OR Ukraine disseminates its own educational program, Don’t Laugh At Me (DLAM), that provides an effective tool for establishing a caring climate in which the emotional and physical abuse children suffer because of peer ridicule, bullying and other asocial behaviors is far less likely to occur.

If you would like to receive materials simply download materials through the ORUkraine website or you may write to all@orukraine.org and request hard copies of the music and lesson plans.

OR Ukraine also offers experienced staff to come and hold trainings in your community to introduce the program (for free!) as well as a trainer’s guide that would allow anyone to hold their own trainings within their community.

If you would like to hold your own training we highly encourage this. Please write to all@orukraine.org and you will be guided on how to do so. Please call Bob at 0500 401 306 or Tommy 0980 243 844 for more information. The Trainers Guide English offers step by step instructions on how to hold a four hour training in your community. The Trainers Presentation English consists of the slides that will be shown during the actual training.
