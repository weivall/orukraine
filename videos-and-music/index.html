Videos and Music

Songs and videos featuring Peter Yarrow, Maria Burmaka, and friends of OR Structured around the song “Don’t Laugh at Me,” Operation Respect uses music to connect with children at a different level that typical lesson plans do not offer. With the help of inspirational songs, the program incorporates the message of the song to the message of Operation Respect. While in Ukraine in December of 2010, Peter Yarrow recorded 5 songs with Ukrainian folk star musician Maria Burmaka. Below are the five songs that were translated and recorded by Maria Burmaka in Ukrainian as well as the songs mixed together in Ukrainian and English. There are also songs by Peter, Paul and Mary and other folk legends.
