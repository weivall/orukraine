Donate

The US dollar goes a long way in Ukraine, a mere $15 dollars covers the cost of one four hour training for 25 teachers. The training introduces all OR materials to a community as well as facilitates a discussion amongst teachers on how to address their specific needs. If you are able to donate please donate through Operation Respect in America. Through the link simply enter the amount you would like to give to OR Ukraine and where it says give to a project type enter “UKRAINE.” Your donation will be tax deductable and 100% will come here to Ukraine. Thank you so much for your support.

We also ask for you to donate you personal experience or story in regards to bullying. Sharing personal stories and is a great way to connect all, and show that we all have been impacted by this issue. Through our common understanding and shared experiences we can move together in stopping bullying and disrespect in our communities. Please email all stories to all@orukraine.org.
